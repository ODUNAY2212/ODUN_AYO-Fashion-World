THE HOUSE HERITAGE

Our Story & Services

A testament to absolute elegance, tailoring heritage, and exquisite materials since inception.

FOUNDING LEGACY

Crafting Nobles Since Inception

Founded with a singular, clear vision: to introduce high-fashion luxury tailoring to Ibadan, Oyo State, while serving clients with exquisite taste nationwide. The name ODUN_AYO represents joy, and we bring this spirit into the creation of every thread, lining, and button.

Our atelier merges classic European cutting techniques with rich African design aesthetics. By focusing heavily on personal measurement calibrations, we ensure that every customer receives garments that represent their stature and posture.

Our Mission

To deliver unparalleled self-confidence through custom-fitting haute couture and bespoke apparel designs.

Our Vision

To stand as Africa's premier luxury fashion destination, matching international houses in craftsmanship and detail.

THE EXPEDITION

Historical Timeline

2018

The First Stitch

Opening a small private studio in Ibadan, dedicated strictly to bespoke gentleman suits and custom bridal measurements.

2020

Heritage Natives Launch

Expanding services to incorporate premium traditional Nigerian native wear, introducing heavy hand-woven Aso-Oke collections.

2023

The Eleyele Showroom

Inaugurating our flagship luxury showroom in Eleyele, Ibadan, featuring dedicated client measuring rooms and private styling lounges.

2026

Digital Atelier launch

Integrating e-commerce logistics to ship customized fitting garments, watches, shoes, and bags to clients globally.
